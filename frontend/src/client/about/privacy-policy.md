# All by All Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 5, 2024

This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information.

We do **not** collect any personal data from our users. No cookies or tracking technologies are used that gather personal information.

## Analytics

We use [Plausible Analytics](https://plausible.io/) to monitor and analyze the usage of our website. Plausible Analytics does not collect any personal data or use cookies.

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
